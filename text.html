Ammamma's Hyderabad Biryani

The best biryani recipe you'll ever find!

A delicious batch of homemade cookies.

Ingredients

  • Chicken – 750 g (with bone preferred)
  • Yogurt (curd) – 1 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 ½ tsp
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Coriander powder – 1 tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Green chilies – 3–4 slit
  • Fresh coriander leaves – ½ cup, chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves – ½ cup, chopped
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Salt – 1 ½ tsp (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, herbs, lemon juice, and half fried onions.
  2. Marinate for 1–2 hours (or overnight).
  3. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  4. Boil water with whole spices and salt.
  5. Cook rice until 70% done.
  6. Drain and keep aside.
  7. Place marinated chicken at the bottom of a heavy pot.
  8. Layer rice on top of chicken.
  9. Add saffron milk, remaining onions, ghee, mint, and coriander.
  10. Seal pot with lid or wheat dough.
  11. Cook on low flame over a tawa for 30–35 minutes.
  12. Or bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  13. Rest for 10 minutes before opening.
  14. Gently mix layers.
  15. Serve hot with raita or salad.
